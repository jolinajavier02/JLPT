The Language

Japanese (Nihongo) is spoken by over 125 million people. It is known for its complex writing system combining Kanji, Hiragana, and Katakana.

The Culture

From the serenity of tea ceremonies to the vibrant energy of anime. Learning the language unlocks a deeper understanding of these traditions.

The Opportunity
