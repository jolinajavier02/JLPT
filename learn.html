Basic Grammar

Sentence Structure: SOV

Japanese uses a Subject-Object-Verb structure.

English: I eat sushi.

Japanese: Watashi wa sushi o tabemasu.

(I [topic] sushi [object] eat)

Essential Particles

は (Wa)

Topic Marker. Indicates what the sentence is about.

を (O)

Object Marker. Indicates the direct object of an action.

が (Ga)

Subject Marker. Identifies the subject of the verb.
